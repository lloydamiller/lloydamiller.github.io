03 : About me

Lloyd Miller is the Chief Technology Officer at Flag Media Analytics where combines his extensive communications experience with a love of building products that help people better achieve their own media goals.

The son of a network engineer, Lloyd started life with a passion for building and connecting technologies that help people. He started a web development company at just 13 years old, helping small businesses get online during the early days of the Internet.

After volunteering to knock on doors for a city council candidate in his home town as a teenager, he saw politics as the best way he could help make a difference. This began a decade and a half of political work. Following years on campaigns across the country, Lloyd started working with non-profits, trade associations, corporate organizations, and poltiical committees to achieve their media and communications goals with in-depth, actionable, and narrative driven research.

However, technology was always his first love, so when struggling to find ways to make the labor-intesive research, media monitoring, and analysis process more efficient, he built his own tools to solve these problems. This led him to Flag Media Analytics, where he oversees product design and development for Flag's suite of products and tools.
